Altas Negras

Altas Negras is a wonderful resort town. As it is California the various beautiful hotels dotting the city can be a bit pricey, some of the cheapest were LaLibra at $160 a night & Fairwhite Inc. at $185 a night. They both Have a great view of the ocean & plenty of rooms too.
